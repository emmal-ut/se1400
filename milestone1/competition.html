My Competition

Who is my competition?

There are probably thousands of online sites that sell art prints, including the thousands of sellers on Etsy.

However, for the area that I am in, and my most likely audience, there are much less options.

Petal Lane is a local shop that sells art prints. They have lot's of different items, and a very cute style. However, they are geared mainly toward home decor.

I would like to be able to offer more than just wall prints. I would like to offer customizable printed items such as holiday cards, invitations, and other printed items.

This is the only place, that I know of, that sells the same types of things in my area.
