Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

My site will contain designs/prints that I have created and are for sale. I would also like this to be a way to inquire other design projects, such as custom cards, invitaitons, etc. This includes images of the products and a way for the user to purchase them.

Where will you get all of the content for your site?

I will create the content. I will use mockups of my designs that would be for sale.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

The focus of the site is to sell my designs. Users are there to shop for, and see the designs.

Why should a site be built for this?

In the world today, people shop online way more often. It is a great way to reach people from all over, not just to sell my art and designs, but also to have people just see what I create.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to view the designs and buy the products.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to reach more people, show my designs, and sell art!
