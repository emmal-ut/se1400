About LuckyLee

Hi, I'm Emma!

Artist, designer, and founder of LuckyLee.

I have always been drawn to creativity — the kind that blends color, emotion, and story into something you can hold in your hands. What started as a love for art and design has grown into a business where I get to do what I love every day: help people bring their ideas to life through beautiful prints, custom designs, and one-of-a-kind art projects.

From wedding invitations to gallery-quality art prints and everything in between, I pour heart and detail into every piece I create. Whether you’re celebrating something big or just want something special made just for you, I’m here to help make it happen.

When I’m not sketching, printing, or dreaming up your next design, you’ll find me; hanging out with my family, friends, and cute dog Chloe, bing watching my favorite tv shows with my favorite treats, traveling or playing outdoors, or working on my own art piece or craft.

My Design Style

My design style is simple, clean, and classy, while still being BOLD. I believe every project should reflect the unique vision of each client. Creating designs that are both visually appealing and authentically tailored to the client and their needs is very important to me. I am intentional in every creation.

Thanks for stopping by — I can’t wait to create something beautiful together!
